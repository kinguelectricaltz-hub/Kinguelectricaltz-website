Our Generator Installation Process

Step 1: Free Site Inspection

Our engineer visits your location in Arusha to:

  • Assess your power requirements
  • Evaluate the installation site
  • Check existing electrical infrastructure
  • Provide a detailed quotation

Step 2: System Design & Planning

We design a custom solution including:

  • Generator sizing (10kVA – 1000kVA)
  • Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) configuration
  • Fuel system planning
  • Exhaust and ventilation requirements

Step 3: Professional Installation

Our certified team handles:

  • Generator positioning and mounting
  • Electrical connections and wiring
  • ATS and control panel installation
  • Fuel system connection
  • Exhaust system fitting
  • Earthing and safety systems

Step 4: Testing & Commissioning

We conduct thorough testing:

  • Full load testing
  • Automatic start/stop testing
  • Transfer time verification
  • Safety system checks
  • Client training on operation and maintenance

Step 5: Handover & Documentation

You receive:

  • Complete installation documentation
  • Operation and maintenance manuals
  • Warranty certificate (1-year)
  • 24/7 emergency support contact
