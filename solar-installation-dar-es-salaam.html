Why Choose Solar Power in Tanzania?

Tanzania receives abundant sunshine throughout the year, making solar power an ideal renewable energy solution. By switching to solar, you can:

  • ☀️ Reduce electricity bills by up to 70%
  • ☀️ Ensure reliable power during grid outages
  • ☀️ Lower your carbon footprint and contribute to a sustainable future
  • ☀️ Protect against rising electricity costs
  • ☀️ Increase property value with a modern solar system

Our Solar Installation Process

Step 1: Free Site Assessment

Our solar experts visit your location in Dar es Salaam to:

  • Assess your energy consumption
  • Evaluate roof space and orientation
  • Check for shading issues
  • Provide a detailed system design and quotation

Step 2: System Design

We design a custom solar solution including:

  • Solar panel selection (monocrystalline/polycrystalline)
  • Inverter sizing (string/hybrid/micro-inverters)
  • Battery storage options (lithium-ion/lead-acid)
  • Mounting structure design
  • Electrical system integration

Step 3: Professional Installation

Our certified team handles:

  • Solar panel mounting and positioning
  • Inverter and battery installation
  • Electrical wiring and connections
  • System grounding and safety measures
  • Grid connection (if applicable)

Step 4: Testing & Commissioning

We conduct thorough testing:

  • System performance verification
  • Safety and compliance checks
  • Client training on system operation
  • System monitoring setup

Step 5: Handover & Support

You receive:

  • Complete system documentation
  • Operation and maintenance manuals
  • Warranty certificate
  • 24/7 support contact
