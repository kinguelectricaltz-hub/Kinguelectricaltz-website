About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2022 by highly Experienced Electrical engineers, Kingu Electrical Company Limited has grown to become a leading electrical engineering and product trading company in Tanzania, serving clients across East Africa with headquarters in Arusha and Dar-es-salaam.

Our Mission

To deliver Exceptional Electrical Engineering Solutions that exceed client expectations while Maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism in all our projects.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Teamwork, and Innovation guide every project we undertake and every relationship we build with our clients across Tanzania.

Generator Services

Solar Systems

Design, installation and maintenance of solar power systems for residential, commercial and industrial applications across Tanzania.

Generator Spare Parts

Supply of genuine diesel generator spare parts and complete generator systems with professional installation services.

Air Conditioning

Professional installation, maintenance, repair and servicing of all types of air conditioning systems for commercial and residential use.

PLC & Automation

Advanced PLC programming, InteliLite & DSE configuration, and industrial automation solutions for telecom and manufacturing sectors.

ATS Systems

HPU Conversion

Technical Training

Electrical Installations

Security & ICT Systems
