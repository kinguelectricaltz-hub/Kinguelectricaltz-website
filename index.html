About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2017 by highly Experienced Electrical Engineers, Kingu Electrical Company Limited has grown to become a leading Electrical Engineering and Product trading company in Tanzania, serving clients across East Africa with headquarters in Arusha and Dar-es-salaam.

Our Mission

To deliver Exceptional Electrical Engineering Solutions that exceed client expectations while Maintaining the highest standards of safety, quality, and professionalism in all our projects.

Our Values

Integrity, Quality, Teamwork, and Innovation guide every project we undertake and every relationship we build with our clients across Tanzania.

Our Services

Generator Services

Complete Generator solutions including installation, Maintenance, service, Repair, Testing and commissioning of diesel generators up to 1000kVA for reliable backup power.

Solar Systems

Design, installation and Maintenance of solar power systems for Residential, Commercial and Industrial Applications across Tanzania.

PLC & Automation

Advanced PLC programming, InteliLite & DSE configuration, and industrial automation solutions for telecom and manufacturing sectors.

CCTV & Security

Professional installation of CCTV systems, access control, and security solutions for homes and businesses.

Electrical Installations

Complete electrical wiring, panel installations, and maintenance services for residential and commercial buildings.

ATS Systems

Design and installation of Automatic Transfer Switch systems for seamless power transition between grid and generators.
